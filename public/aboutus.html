Small Blue Idea, LLC.

We believe the future of business is in custom software solutions designed specifically for your business and for your team. Not big box solutions designed for everyone that work for no one.

Our mission is to help speed up broken, inefficient processes and help manufacturers leverage transformative new technologies in Generative AI, Robotics, Process Automation, and Software Design.
