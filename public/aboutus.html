About Us

Small Blue Idea, LLC.

Small Blue Idea, LLC. was founded in 2019 by a small group of engineers who wanted to create a company that would empower businesses to do more with less. We believe that the future of business productivity is in automation, and that the future of automation is in generative AI, digital simulation, robotics, and custom software solutions that put the business at the center of its design. We believe that when businesses empower employees with the use of these new tools, not only will they do more with less, they will do what they do better.

Our mission is to help companies bridge the gap between transformative new technologies and their practical business processes, while providing custom software solutions that are focused on simplicity, elegance, and functionality. We believe that the best solutions are a reflection of the people who created it, and so we strive to make each and every solution a reflection of our drive, our passion, and our commitment to excellence.
